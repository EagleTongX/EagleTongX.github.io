# Product Analyze Exercise Day 1 -- VIDEOASK by Typeform

**Brief View of This App**:

*At first glance, I thought this app was a social video application which you can  send a short video or voice to your friends. But it seems wrong when I started to explore it.*

VIDEOASK is where you can record a short video (30s) for you question and share the link with others to gather the answers. The answer can be in three different forms, video, voice and text. 

**WorkFlow:**

1. Free for download from Appstore (only ios version now)
2. No sign in or sign up requirement
3. Short introduction when openning the app first time
4. Move to the recording page directly after asking for permission
5. You can choose from video or voice to record your question
6. Choose the type of the answers, video, voice, text or multiple choice(poll)
7. Name your VideoAsk
8. Good to go! You will get the link or share using other platform.
9. You can review your videoask and download the responses, all responses will be generate to text form. 

*Apart from mobile app, VideoAsk can be embeded into your own website, which is also their initial target customers.*

**What Makes Them Successful?**

The World Of Data. All the innovation, evaluation and development are based on data. Collecting data is a common problem for different people in different industry. We can see questioniare, survey, free-street customer interview everywhere. How to make this process easier and more efficient? How to get more people involved and gather more answers(data)? 

VideoAsk gives an answer. They provide a simple way to get feedback. At the same time increase the interaction between the publisher and the visitors. 

I first think about the "ask a question" feature on Instagram stories, this is somehow some connections between these two. But no one put the video contact form into the formal occassion before. 

Obviously, simple process is another factor making it successful.  Reduce the sign in process both for publishers and visitors to make it efficient and focus on the main purpose. 

**What Did They Get Right?**

Gathering feedback is a short term requirment. People do not carry out survey everyday. It only happen when you have some hypothesis to test or you want to hear some feedback for your product. VideoAsk makes it accessible as a plug-in on your website (and maybe some other platform later), which you can create a contact form greeting video. So the initial target customer is more clear.

Also, besides video they provide voice and text choices in order to solve the problem that user are not comfortable with face-to-face contact. 

**How Did The People Behind This Product Think About This Idea?**

Target Customer: 

Website owner who want to increase the visiting rate and subsistance

Startup who wants to get feedback from users or test business hypothesis

Market researchers, students or anyone who wants to collect data to validate or support their argument.
