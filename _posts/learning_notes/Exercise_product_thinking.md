# Learning notes about how to exercise product mindset

**Exercise Your Product Mindset**:

Go to Product Hunt every day, pick top 3 products:

- What makes them successful?
- What did they get right?
- How did the people behind this product think about this idea?

Think more:

*Think about the person that made this decision*

- What constraints were they under 
- What other data points do they have that could explain their decision?
- Who is their target user? Could it by any chance not be me?
- Are they testing something? If so, what?

— Paul Cothenet <u>Exercise Your Product Muscles</u>

**Analyze Products**:

*Something to consider before exploring the app*:

(have a better understanding of the app's value proposition)

- how did this app come to your attention?
  - What compelled you to go find it? how many times your heard it before?
- what's your one-line summary of what this app does at this stage?
  - Gain the insight of your first impression and compare the answer after using the app
- what's the buzz so far?
  - Do you have a sense of whether it's popular? what the description, rating , reviews of that?

*what to think when analyzing the app?*

- What's the experience of getting started or signing up?
- How does this app explain itself in the first minute?
  - Was it clear or confusing? Did you read and retain the information?
- How easy to use was the app? 
  - Understand immediately or need to try things and figure it out?
- How did you feel when exploring the app?
- Did the app deliver on your expectations?
  - If it's a content app, was the content engaging? If it's a utility app, did it solve the identified problem in a meaningful manner?
- How long did you spend using the app?

*Thinking About the stickiness and growth*:

- How often have you used the app? when do you tend to use it?
- How does this app compare to other similar apps?
  - which better or worse? Why you choose this versus others?

- What do other people think of this app?
  - Read reviews, comments and ask people around you

- Based on what you know, how successful you think this app can achieve in one year? 
- Is your prediction right? how your personal preference and tastes similar or different from people in the broader market?

—Julie Zhuo <u>How to do a product critique</u>
